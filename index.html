About

Emily Foley is a junior full-stack web developer based in San Francisco, building awesome stuff with Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and jQuery. She enjoys back-end work and is learning more front-end, and focusing especially on learning Node.js. Her favorite pastime is to go on adventures, mainly outdoors and preferably with her partner in crime, Joe.

Projects

  • Hat Tip

    HatTip is a Rails application I built with a team of 4 other students at Dev Bootcamp in San Francisco. HatTip mashes up the YouTube, Facebook, Coinbase and Stripe APIs so that visitors can view amateur music and dance performances and virtually tip those they like.

  • Kard Kata

    Kard Kata is a Sinatra based flashcard-study application I built with another team of Dev Bootcamp students, using a PostgreSQL database.
